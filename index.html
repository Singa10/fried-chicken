At Singa Fried Chicken, we believe great food tells a story—and ours begins with a legendary rooster and a sizzling dream. Born in the heart of Addis Ababa, Singa Fried Chicken blends bold East African spirit with crispy, golden perfection. Our secret? A signature spice mix inspired by local flavors, paired with a crunch that keeps you coming back for more. We’re not just serving fried chicken—we’re serving comfort, community, and a little bit of magic in every bite. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or sharing a bucket with friends, Singa is where flavor meets soul.
